Case 01

Lead Response Automation

Problem

Businesses manually respond to website enquiries, causing slow replies and lost leads before the prospect moves on.

Automation Workflow
  1. Form submission triggers automation in Make.com
  2. AI classifies lead intent from form data
  3. Personalised email response generated and sent
  4. Lead contact stored automatically in CRM
  5. Team notified with lead summary via Slack or email
Outcome

Replies sent in seconds instead of hours. No leads go cold.

Case 02

Email Inbox Triage Automation

Problem

Customer support teams spend hours every day manually sorting and responding to incoming emails — a repetitive task that slows response times.

Automation Workflow
  1. New email detected in shared inbox
  2. AI reads and categorises the request type
  3. Email automatically routed to correct team or folder
  4. AI-generated reply suggestion surfaced for agent review
Outcome

Email admin reduced significantly. Teams focus on decisions, not sorting.

Case 03

Client Onboarding Automation

Problem

Manual onboarding processes create delays, missing documents, and inconsistent client experiences that require significant admin time.

Automation Workflow
  1. Client signup or payment triggers the sequence
  2. Personalised welcome email sent immediately
  3. Document requests collected via automated form
  4. Internal tasks created in project management tool
Outcome

Faster onboarding and a smoother client experience with no missed steps.

Case 04

Invoice Processing Automation

Problem

Finance teams manually process incoming invoices — reading, extracting data, and entering it into accounting software — consuming hours of repetitive work.

Automation Workflow
  1. Invoice email received in designated inbox
  2. AI parses document to extract amounts, dates, and vendor
  3. Structured data pushed to accounting software
  4. Approval workflow triggered for finance team review
Outcome

Finance admin time drastically reduced. Processing happens in minutes, not hours.
