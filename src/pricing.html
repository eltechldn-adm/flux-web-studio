FAQ

Common Questions

Answers to questions we receive before most projects begin.

Can I request a custom automation not listed here? +

Yes. The packages above cover the most common use cases, but if your workflow is more specific, submit a request and describe what you need. We will assess it and provide a fixed quote before any work begins.

Do I need any technical knowledge to get started? +

No technical knowledge is needed. You only need to describe the workflow you want automated in plain language. We handle all configuration, integration, and testing. You do not need to understand Make.com or any other tool to work with us.

Can automation connect to my existing tools? +

In most cases, yes. We build with Make.com, which connects to hundreds of common business tools including Gmail, Google Sheets, Airtable, Notion, Slack, and most CRMs. Confirm your tools when submitting a request and we will check compatibility before committing to the project.

What happens after the automation is delivered? +

Every project includes written documentation and handover guidance. Your team will know how to operate and monitor the automation independently. If you need changes later, you can submit a new request. Modifications are scoped as a separate project at a fixed price.

Is payment taken upfront or on delivery? +

Payment is taken in full before work begins. This allows us to prioritise your project immediately and commit to the agreed delivery timeline without delays.
